
# OptiRFP Known Limitations

While we continually work to improve OptiRFP, there are some current limitations to be aware of:

## Document Analysis

- **File Size Limit**: RFP documents must be under 50MB
- **Supported Formats**: Currently limited to PDF, DOC, DOCX, and TXT files
- **Complex Tables**: The AI may struggle with extracting complex table data from PDFs
- **Image Recognition**: Our system cannot extract information from images within documents
- **Language Support**: Optimal performance with English language RFPs; limited support for other languages

## AI Generation Capabilities

- **Specialized Industry Knowledge**: While our AI has broad knowledge, very niche industry terms or concepts may require manual input
- **Technical Specifications**: Highly technical specifications may need expert review
- **Response Length**: Single section generation is optimized for up to 1000 words
- **Iteration Limits**: Each section can be regenerated up to 5 times per day on Starter plan (unlimited on Pro)

## Project Management

- **Project Limits**: 
  - Trial: 3 projects
  - Starter: 10 projects
  - Pro: 30 projects
- **Collaboration**: Currently limited to single-user access per account (team collaboration coming soon)
- **File Storage**: 500MB total storage on Trial, 5GB on Starter, 20GB on Pro

## Export Capabilities

- **Export Formats**: Currently limited to DOCX and PDF
- **Custom Formatting**: Limited formatting options for exported documents
- **Image Handling**: Maximum of 20 images per exported document

## Performance Considerations

- **Generation Time**: AI generation may take 30-90 seconds depending on complexity
- **Concurrent Operations**: Performance may degrade when running multiple AI operations simultaneously
- **Browser Support**: Optimized for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge; older browsers may have limited functionality

## Upcoming Improvements

We're actively working on addressing these limitations in future updates:
- Team collaboration features
- Enhanced export formatting options
- Expanded language support
- Image recognition capabilities
- Advanced table extraction
